# Sample questions (known to work against banking.db)

These are verified against the current schema and data volumes
(1,000 customers / 1,500 accounts / 50,000 transactions / 400 loans / 15 branches).

1. What's the total transaction amount per month in 2024?
2. Which 5 branches have the highest total account balance?
3. Break down active loans by loan type.
4. What's the average account balance by customer income bracket?
5. How many customers joined each year?
6. Which region has the most branches?
7. What's the total loan amount disbursed per year?
8. Show the top 10 customers by total account balance.
9. What percentage of loans are in default, by loan type?
10. What's the average transaction amount by category?
11. Which account type holds the most total balance?
12. How many transactions were credits vs debits in 2024?
